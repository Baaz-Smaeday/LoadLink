# 💰 LoadLink — Business Plan

## Market Opportunity

### The Problem

The UK same-day courier market is worth **£10+ billion** annually, yet the dominant platform (Courier Exchange) has:

- **Tripled prices** since 2015 (now £2,300+/year for drivers)
- **12-month lock-in contracts** with no refunds
- **Only 1 booking mode** (quotes) — no instant booking
- **3 separate apps** instead of a unified experience
- **No direct business access** — businesses must go through intermediaries
- **Poor customer service** — slow ticket-based support

### Target Market

| Segment | UK Market Size | Our Target (Year 1) |
|---------|---------------|---------------------|
| Owner Drivers | ~50,000 active | 500-1,000 |
| Courier Companies | ~5,000 | 100-200 |
| Freight Forwarders | ~3,000 | 50-100 |
| Direct Businesses | Millions | 1,000-5,000 |

### Why Now?

1. **Driver frustration at all-time high** — CX pricing backlash across forums
2. **Businesses expect Uber-like experience** — instant booking, live tracking
3. **Technology is cheaper than ever** — we can build what CX has for a fraction of the cost
4. **Post-pandemic delivery boom** — same-day delivery expectations are permanent

## Revenue Model

### Revenue Streams

| Stream | Who Pays | Amount | When |
|--------|---------|--------|------|
| Transaction fee | Businesses | 8-12% per booking | Every booking |
| Driver Pro subscription | Drivers | £49-79/month | Monthly |
| Driver commission (free tier) | Drivers | 8% per completed job | Every job |
| Forwarder subscription | Freight Forwarders | £79-149/month | Monthly |
| Fleet seats | Courier Companies | £29-49/vehicle/month | Monthly |
| Instant payout fee | Drivers | 2-3% per payout | Per payout |
| Featured listings | All users | £10-30/week | Weekly |
| API access | Enterprise | Custom pricing | Monthly |

### Pricing Philosophy

- **Businesses**: No subscription — pay per use only. Removes all barriers.
- **Drivers**: Choose between subscription (unlimited, no commission) or free tier (8% commission). Always cheaper than CX.
- **Everyone**: No annual lock-in. Cancel anytime.

## Go-to-Market Strategy

### Phase 1: Driver Acquisition (Month 1-3)

**Target**: Get 200+ drivers on the platform

- **CX Frustration Marketing** — Target drivers on CX forums, Facebook groups, Reddit
- **Free Tier** — £0 to join, only pay 8% when you earn. Zero risk.
- **Driver referral programme** — Refer a driver, both get 1 month free Pro
- **Direct outreach** — Contact van driver communities, WhatsApp groups

### Phase 2: Business Acquisition (Month 2-6)

**Target**: Get 500+ businesses booking regularly

- **Google Ads** — Target "urgent courier", "same day delivery", "van hire"
- **Industry targeting** — Direct outreach to manufacturing, legal, healthcare, events
- **No-account booking** — One-off bookings with just an email. Frictionless.
- **Price comparison** — Show savings vs traditional courier companies

### Phase 3: Network Effects (Month 6+)

More businesses → more loads → drivers earn more → more drivers join → faster pickup times → better service → more businesses

### Key Channels

1. **SEO / Content** — "How much does same-day courier cost?", "Courier Exchange alternatives"
2. **Google Ads** — High-intent keywords
3. **Social Media** — Driver success stories, business case studies
4. **Partnerships** — Van insurance companies, fuel card providers, vehicle dealers
5. **Industry events** — Transport & logistics trade shows

## Competitive Advantages

### vs Courier Exchange

| Advantage | Impact |
|-----------|--------|
| 3 booking modes vs 1 | More flexible, faster bookings |
| 80% cheaper for drivers | Massive switching incentive |
| No annual lock-in | Lower risk to try |
| Direct business access | New market CX doesn't serve |
| One unified app | Better user experience |
| Modern technology | Faster, more reliable, better mobile |
| Customer tracking links | Feature CX doesn't have |

### Moat (Long-term Defensibility)

1. **Network effects** — More users = more value for everyone
2. **Data advantage** — Real-time pricing data, route optimisation
3. **Brand loyalty** — Built BY frustrated CX users, FOR the industry
4. **Technology lead** — Modern stack vs CX's legacy systems
5. **Switching costs** — Once businesses and drivers build history/ratings, they stay

## Key Metrics to Track

| Metric | What It Tells Us |
|--------|-----------------|
| **GMV** (Gross Merchandise Value) | Total value of bookings through platform |
| **Take rate** | Our revenue as % of GMV |
| **DAU/MAU** | Daily/monthly active users |
| **Time to first quote** | Platform responsiveness |
| **Booking conversion** | % of loads that get booked |
| **Driver retention** | Monthly churn rate |
| **NPS** | Net Promoter Score — would users recommend us? |
| **CAC** | Cost to acquire a customer |
| **LTV** | Lifetime value per customer |
| **LTV:CAC ratio** | Must be >3:1 for sustainable growth |
