Non-Compete Traps

LoadLink's Answer
Escrow-protected payments, 4% commission with caps, no lock-ins, and a trust system that rewards good behaviour on both sides.
How It Works

Four steps to
getting paid safely

Whether you're a driver finding work or a business booking deliveries, LoadLink makes it simple and secure.

Post or Find
Businesses post jobs with clear pricing. Drivers browse, filter, and accept or counter-offer.
Escrow Locked
Payment is locked in escrow before the driver moves. Your money is guaranteed.
Deliver & Prove
GPS tracking, photos, POD signatures. Everything logged for trust and disputes.
Get Paid
Invoice approved, payment released instantly or on terms. No chasing. No excuses.
